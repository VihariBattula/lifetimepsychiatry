Our Dedicated Providers

Dr. Srinivas Battula

Board-Certified Psychiatrist

Dr. Srinivas Battula earned his medical degree from Siddhartha Medical College in India, followed by a residency in Psychiatry at the University of Maryland Medical System (UMMS) & Sheppard Pratt Hospital in Baltimore, Maryland.

He completed his fellowship in Consultation Liaison Psychiatry at the University of Maryland in Baltimore. Dr. Battula has been in the practice of psychiatry since 1997 and is board-certified in Psychiatry.

Areas of Expertise

• Anxiety Disorders • Bipolar Disorder
• Depression • Psychotic Disorders

Dr. Chinya Murali

Board-Certified in General & Geriatric Psychiatry

Dr. Chinya Murali obtained his medical degree from M.S. Ramaiah Medical College in India followed by residency in Psychiatry at the National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences in India and residency in Psychiatry at the University of Chicago Hospitals in Chicago, IL in 1999.

Our Specialized Services

We offer a comprehensive range of psychiatric services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. To book a consultation with one of our dedicated staff and to see how we can provide help to you or your loved ones, please book a consultation meeting with us.

General Psychiatry

Comprehensive assessment and management of mental health conditions for adults. We treat a wide range of conditions including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, PTSD, OCD, and ADHD.

Our approach includes medication management, psychotherapy, and lifestyle recommendations tailored to each individual's needs.

Geriatric Psychiatry

Specialized care focused on the mental and emotional health of older adults. This includes dementia care, mood disorders in the elderly, late-life schizophrenia, and management of behavioral symptoms.

We understand the unique challenges of aging and provide compassionate care that respects the dignity of our senior patients.

Telehealth Visits

Convenient video consultations from the comfort and privacy of your home. Available for follow-up appointments and select new patient evaluations.

Our secure telehealth platform allows you to receive quality psychiatric care without leaving your home, making treatment more accessible and convenient.

Medication Management

Comprehensive evaluation and monitoring of psychiatric medications to ensure optimal treatment outcomes with minimal side effects.

We work closely with each patient to find the right medication regimen, providing education and ongoing support throughout treatment.

Second Opinion

Comprehensive diagnostic review and treatment recommendations for patients seeking additional perspectives on their mental health care.

We provide thorough evaluations to help patients make informed decisions about their treatment options.
